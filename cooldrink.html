Write-up On Meal

A cooldrink is a drink that usually contains water, a sweetener, and a natural and/or artificial flavoring.
The sweetener may be a sugar, high-fructose corn syrup, fruit juice, a sugar substitute, or some combination of these. Soft drinks may also contain caffeine, colorings, preservatives, and/or other ingredients.
